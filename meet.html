Meet the panel

Barnsley Council's Stronger Communities service works alongside other partners to be the catalyst for social change and to improve the quality of life across the Barnsley Borough. It is committed to fostering resilient and capable communities and to building capacity among citizens, enabling them to contribute to a brighter future and a better Barnsley. The service encompasses six Area Teams that are responsible for area governance, commissioning arrangements, and community engagement. Their work is significant in early intervention and prevention, contributing to the health and well-being of the community.

Wates are a environmentally-minded construction company that reimagines places for people to thrive. We have a strong legacy of quality, safety and social value. We want to lead the way in creating a built environment that can unlock human potential, improve health and wellbeing, and shape future prospects. We have a new purpose that guides everything we do: to change lives and strengthen communities in ways few other industries can.

Berneslai Homes is a social housing provider that manages around 18,000 homes on behalf of Barnsley Council. They support tenants and communities with a range of services, from repairs and maintenance to money advice and skills development. They are committed to meeting the standards of the Regulator of Social Housing and working with Barnsley Council to build and acquire new homes.
