About us

The Better Barnsley Bond is a social value fund fueled by contributions from contractors working on the Town Centre development. We believe in fostering possibilities, where creativity and vision thrive. Whether you're focused on community development, education, the arts, or innovative research, we're committed to supporting initiatives that make a positive impact.

What are we looking for?

We seek applications that align with our shared vision of continuing the transformation of Barnsley as a place of opportunities. Your proposal should illustrate how it supports our priorities: fostering a healthy, growing, learnig, and sustainable Barnsley. Additionally, please consider how you will ensure the long-term viability of your project.

Who can apply?

  • The voluntary and community sector
  • Community groups
  • Faith-based organisations
  • Youth clubs
  • Charities
  • Community Interest Companies
